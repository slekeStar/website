About Us

The Station is a family-owned hobby shop specializing in model trains and train sets. We offer N, HO, and O scale options, along with parts, accessories, and expert repair services. Our knowledgeable staff is passionate about model railroading and dedicated to helping you build the layout of your dreams.

Available Scales

N Scale

1:160

Perfect for detailed layouts in limited space. Popular for creating expansive scenes with intricate detail.

HO Scale

1:87

The most popular scale worldwide. Offers excellent detail and a wide variety of available products.

O Scale

1:48

Large and impressive models with exceptional detail. Traditional American favorite for serious collectors.
